# App screenshots for the hero phone slideshow

Drop real app screenshots here and the hero phone auto-plays them as a slideshow.
Until at least two exist, the phone shows the invoice-PDF fallback instead.

## Expected files (PNG or JPG works if you rename to .png)
- `home.png`      — the Home screen (with your logo)
- `invoices.png`  — the Invoices list
- `cards.png`     — the Business Card screen
- `profile.png`   — the Profile screen

Order shown: home → invoices → cards → profile. To add/rename/reorder, edit the
`order` array in the slideshow script at the bottom of `../../index.html`.

## How to capture (best done on your phone, where the app has your real data)
1. Open the installed Inkvoice app (or app.elorate.net) on your phone.
2. Go to each screen and take a normal screenshot.
3. Crop out the phone's status bar if you like (optional — the frame hides edges).
4. Rename to the names above and copy them into this folder.

Phone screenshots are already the right shape, so they fill the frame with no crop.
